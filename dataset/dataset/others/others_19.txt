Muslim Ladies College, Bambalapitiya, Colombo organized its Founder's Day Ceremony at the College Hajara Hall recently. It was held under the patronage of the Principal of the College, Rasmia Aboobucker.
Muslim Ladies College is known and recognized in Sri Lanka as the premier state educational institution for Muslim girls. The school was officially reopened on November 1, 1946 by Minister of Education the late C.W. W. Kannangara with 26 students and three teachers. Presently 3500 students and 130 teachers serve the school. Vice Principals, teachers, members of the Past Pupils Association and students were present at the ceremony.
