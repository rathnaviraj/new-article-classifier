Altair was the sponsor of the 146th Annual General Meeting and dinner of the Colombo Club, one of the oldest social clubs in Asia.
As a part of its support to this elegant event, Altair sponsored two Business Class return tickets to Singapore and several other prizes presented to the winners of a raffle draw for members of the club.
This was the second consecutive year that Altair was associated with this prestigious organisation as the sponsor of its AGM. Here presentation one of the prizes by Head of Business Development at Altair,Mumtaz Niyasdeen.
